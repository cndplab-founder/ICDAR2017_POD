Protocol

Regisration


To participate in this competition, please send an e-mail to chlxyd@pku.edu.cn with the subject "ICDAR 2017 POD Competition Registration". Following information are required for registration.
  • Group name
  • Institution
  • Participants and e-mail
  • Contact person
  • Target Tasks(Maybe more than 1)

    • We will reply a feedback e-mail to the participants. When the competition start, the password of dataset will be sent to the contact person.

    Submission


    Participantss are required to compress the results XML file into ZIP format, send the results to chlxyd@pku.edu.cn with the subject "(Your Group Name)ICDAR 2017 POD Competition Submission (Your submission ID)", you can send different results for many times before the submit deadline, but we will only regard the last email as your results.

    For any questions, please contact chlxyd@pku.edu.cn.
